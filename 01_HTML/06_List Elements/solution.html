Mountain Trekking

Preparation

Luggage

  • Backpack
  • Sleeping bag
  • Tent
  • Cooking stove
  • Utensils
  • First aid kit

For tent

  • 1 tent
  • 1 sleeping bag
  • 1 cooking stove

Instructions

  1. Pack all necessary items into your backpack.
  2. Set up your tent at the campsite.
  3. Use the cooking stove to prepare meals.
  4. Keep the first aid kit accessible for emergencies.
