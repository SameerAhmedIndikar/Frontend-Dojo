First pillar of DOM

Structure

The first pillar of DOM is Structure. The DOM represents a document as a tree of nodes. Each node represents a part of the document, such as an element, attribute, or piece of text. The tree structure allows developers to navigate and manipulate the document easily.

DOM tree

Document

    │   └── title
    │       └── "DOM:First Pillar"
    └── body
        ├── h1
        │   └── "First pillar of DOM"
        └── h2
        │   └── "Structure" 
        └── p
            └── "The first pillar......"
