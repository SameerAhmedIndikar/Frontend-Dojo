Encapsulation in JavaScript

Encapsulation is one of the fundamental principles of Object-Oriented Programming. It refers to the bundling of data (properties) and methods (functions) that operate on the data into a single unit or class, and restricting direct access to some of the object's components. This helps protect the internal state and provides a clear interface.
